The Walk-In Archive. Every surveyed room of Potter Creek and Samwel at true scale in one space — the arched entrance, the 42 ft great pit, the great room and its breccia fans, the buried galleries, then across to Samwel: Pleistocene Hall, the Gate, Chamber One, Merriam’s Chamber, and down the ~90 ft drop to Chamber Two and the sacred Magic Pool. Rooms stream in and dither out of the dark as you near them. Drag to look; arrow keys / WASD to move (FLY for the vertical drops). Each label cites its survey; the cross-cave corridor is flagged interpretive.
