Honest 3-D at Machine Speed

Provenance-disciplined, standards-conformant X3D, authored by a human–AI pair. Two Pleistocene fossil caves rebuilt to scale, a rigged HAnim character pipeline, and a reusable toolchain — every scene procedural and to scale, every drawing a faithful trace of a public-domain original. No AI imagery, no invented geometry.

Toolchain

X3D MCP — render backend + validatorsPR #10 The X_ITE render backend, X3DUOM semantic validators, and autofix, contributed upstream to the Web3D x3d_mcp.

Built by a human and Claude (Claude Code) working as a pair, entirely through the open Web3D toolchain. Both caves are Winnemem Wintu sacred sites on the McCloud River, flooded by Shasta Dam (1945); they are treated with care, not as scenery, and the protected location is not published. Renders use X_ITE.
