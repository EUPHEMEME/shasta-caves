Samwel Cave — Wintu sawal (glossed both "sacred place" and the word for grizzly bear), the Cave of the Lost Maiden. McCloud River, Shasta County. Surveyed by Furlong, Merriam & Sinclair (1903–06); cross-section after Feranec et al. (2007). Now on the shore of Shasta Lake, on flooded Winnemem Wintu homeland. Chamber layout and the Magic Pools are documented, and a deep drop joins the two levels; the ~90 ft "hole" is from legend. Room sizes, pit geometry, speleothems and lighting are interpretive.
